# AIDD Task 9 & 10: Book Generation Skills

## Overview

This task involves the creation of custom AI skills designed to assist in the book generation process. These skills leverage the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate and enhance various aspects of creative writing, from outlining to editing.

## Implemented Skills

### 1. Chapter Outline Generator

* **Purpose:** Generates a detailed, structured outline for a book chapter based on the overall plot and character arcs.
* **Key Features:** Scene breakdown, pacing control, and plot point integration.

### 2. Character Development Assistant

* **Purpose:** Helps flesh out characters by generating backstories, personality traits, and motivations.
* **Key Features:** Character questionnaires, arc planning, and relationship mapping.

### 3. Plot Consistency Checker

* **Purpose:** Analyzes the narrative for contradictions, plot holes, and timeline inconsistencies.
* **Key Features:** Timeline tracking, fact verification within the story world, and character consistency checks.

### 4. Research and Fact-Checking Tool

* **Purpose:** Assists writers in gathering accurate information for their stories, ensuring plausibility and realism.
* **Key Features:** Rapid information retrieval, source citation, and context-aware fact-checking.

### 5. Grammar and Style Enhancer

* **Purpose:** Improves the prose by suggesting stylistic improvements, correcting grammar, and enhancing readability.
* **Key Features:** Tone adjustment, vocabulary enhancement, and structural editing.
